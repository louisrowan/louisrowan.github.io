Hippos


About

This website is about the coolest animal around, the Hippopotamus. Hippos are among the most feared animals in all of Africa, due to their giant size, teeth and ferocity. They are the third-largest land animal behind only the Elephant and Rhinoceros, and can live in the wild for as long as 50 years. They spend up to 16 hours per day in water, a necessity in the African heat to keep their bodies cool.

Hippos live in groups called schools, which usually consist of 10-30 hippos, one of which is the dominant male.

Quick Facts:

  1. Live in Sub-Saharan Africa
  2. Can weigh up to 9,000 pounds
  3. Can run up to 19 mph
  4. Hippos eat up to 80 pounds of grass every night
